name: Critical Thinking Guardrails
description: Miles's critical thinking is degrading from AI offloading; agents must protect it, not replace it
type: constraint
---

Miles has explicitly flagged that his critical thinking capacity is going down (AI cognitive offloading, per APA 2026 / MIT 2025 studies referenced in his own UPath Epistemology brief).

Every agent output, dashboard suggestion, and resource pick must:
1. Show the work — reveal reasoning + sources, not just polished answers.
2. Name uncertainty explicitly — say what we do NOT know.
3. Never do the thinking FOR him on identity, strategy, or decisions. Ask foundational questions instead (What's the real problem? What's your hypothesis? What are your core assumptions? What alternatives exist?).
4. Translate numeracy — never present a stat without denominator + real-world implication.
5. Cross-verify sources (wire → news → in-depth → grey lit → academic → books).

The CriticalThinkingGuard component on the Dashboard rotates one foundational-question prompt per day so Miles stays in the driver's seat of his own thinking.
